Awesome Cookie Recipe

Ultimant Chippers

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter softened
  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. In medium bowl combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Beat butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar in large bowl until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
  5. Add Flour mixture to butter mixture and beat until well blended.
  6. Stir in the chips and pecans, if desired.
  7. Drop dough by heaping teaspoonfuls 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheet(s).
  8. Bake 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
  9. Let cookies stand on cookie sheets 2 minutes and then move to wire racks to cool completely
