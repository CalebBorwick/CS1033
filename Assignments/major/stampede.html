Calgary Stampede 

Come see the Greatest Outdoor Show on Earth. This ten day long celebration features midway rides, rodeo, concerts and much more. This event has been running since 1912 and helps to celebrate Calgaries western heritage, culture and community.

Rodeo

Come enjoy nine different rodeo events from barrel racing to bull riding this event has something for everyone. If your a competator there is over $2 Million in prize money. Events include:

  • Barrel Racing
  • Bareback Riding
  • Tie Down Roping
  • Steer Westling
  • Saddle Bronc
  • Bull Riding 
  • Bareback

Each with different age categories.
