Welcome to Calgary 

Home to ~1.3 million people and the centre of the Canadian Oil Industry, Calgary or cowtown is where I call home. Calgary is known for its booming Oil and Gas industry but it is also known for its western culture specifically the Calgary Stampede. Calgary is centered around the Bow and Elbow River with the city spanning far and wide and still growing. With the city being the 4th largest in Canada there are tons of things to do check out the site seeing for more information. Calgary was founded in 1875 and has been growing exponentially but has always kept its heratage close clearly evident through the success of Heratage Park.
